About

Unknown Yard

A concept conceived and introduced to a secluded street of Berlin in 2022

Our aim upon creation was to provide locals and international couples with an escape from the city, without leaving the city.


Situated in the midst of the metropolis, at the core of German culture, yet barely noticeable from the exterior, it is almost as if this romantic retreat has always been here. The building blends in to Berlin and provides an undisturbed space for guests to mute the madness for a much-needed moment.

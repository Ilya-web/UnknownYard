About

Unknown Yard: a small and cosy boutique hotel nestled on a secluded street of Berlin

An intimate location, where privacy is our priority. Mute the madness and enjoy one another for just a few hours, or settle down together for a night in one of our 11 restful rooms


Take a break from the rush of the city centre to refuel, reset and revitalise in our small spa, lounge area, summer time terrace or cafe, or simply shut yourselves away in ultimate secrecy.
